Department of CSE

Computer Science and Engineering

The Department of Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) has been a part of SRM Valliammai Engineering College since the institution's inception in 1999. The B.E. CSE program initially began with an intake of 60 students and has grown significantly over the years. Recognizing the rising global demand for skilled engineers, the intake was increased to 180 in the academic year 2019-2020. The B.E. CSE program has also been accredited by the National Board of Accreditation (NBA) for a period of three years.

To further strengthen advanced learning and research, the department introduced a postgraduate program — M.E. Computer Science and Engineering — with an intake of 18 students starting from the academic year 2012-2013.

About the Department

Vision

To adopt innovative, engaging, and impactful teaching practices that uncover the inherent talents and competencies of young minds, enabling them to evolve into skilled, ethical, and humane Computer Science Engineering professionals.

Mission

• To provide students with challenging learning opportunities
• To foster practical knowledge & research excellence
• To enhance societal well-being through industry-institute collaboration

Objective

To uphold excellence in the teaching-learning process and help students acquire deep knowledge and strong professional skills in Computer Science and Engineering.

Long-Term Goals

  • Conduct international conferences
  • Obtain government/multinational funded projects
  • Establish MoUs with global companies
  • Start an approved Research Centre

Short-Term Goals

  • Promote R&D activities
  • Improve academics to 100% result
  • Publish papers in reputed journals
  • Train using latest technologies
  • Motivate students to do internships
